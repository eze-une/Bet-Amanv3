About Us

Beyond charity 

Our organization was created with the goal of supporting struggling families, but we go beyond providing just financial and resource assistance. We strive to establish long-lasting relationships with families and children, ensuring that they receive proper education, mental and emotional support. Our efforts aim to create bonds that extend beyond one-time support, fostering a sense of community and lasting impact

Our Mission

we strive to be a partner in our Community's struggles and being present during times of social happiness and trials, all while maintaining a steadfast commitment to strengthening relationships within the context of Orthodox Christianity."
