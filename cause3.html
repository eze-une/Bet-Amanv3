Donating Blood Supporting Children's Educational journey

we have partnered with Abnet School to support the educational journey of children in our community. We provide financial assistance to families who cannot afford the school fees, uniforms, and other educational materials. Additionally, we organize mentoring programs and workshops to help children develop their skills and interests. We believe that education is key to breaking the cycle of poverty and providing children with a brighter future. By supporting Abnet School, we are investing in the future of our community and helping children reach their full potential.
